# Benefits of Using Markdown Editors like Zettlr – By Pranav Bhardwaj

Zettlr is a powerful open-source Markdown editor that supports Zettelkasten note-taking, academic writing, and everyday journaling. Here's why I find it worth using.

## 📝 Key Points

- Fully open-source and privacy-focused.
- Supports citations, LaTeX, and academic workflows.
- Zettelkasten-friendly with internal linking (`[[link]]`) support.
- Minimalist UI, distraction-free writing.
- Works offline, no account needed.

## 🔍 Why I Prefer Zettlr

Zettlr hits the sweet spot between functionality and simplicity. Unlike heavy apps like Obsidian or Notion, Zettlr doesn’t lock you in. It stores everything in plain `.md` files, meaning your notes are future-proof and portable.

I use it for:

- 🧠 Zettelkasten method-based note linking
- ✍️ Writing blog drafts and technical articles
- 📚 Research organization with BibTeX support
